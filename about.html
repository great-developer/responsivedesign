About Us

We are a global team of industry experts, academia and space enthusiasts. With roots in the University of Oxford, we have access to experts and latest research. Our team has rich technical expertise in areas of artificial intelligence, machine learning, image and signal analysis, satellite remote sensing, and delivering software solutions to established organisations. The team also boasts of management expertise with few team members part of Said Business School at University of Oxford. Our diverse team with a complementing skill set helps us solve any problem and achieve goals that we set.

Our Vision

We strive to be the leader in creating sustainable and scalable space-based software solutions with advanced AI and Blockchain capabilities for satellites, rockets and space habitats.

Mission #1

Make satellites smarter by enabling edge data processing and analytics on orbit using highly optimized ML models to generate near real-time insights for applications such as detection of wildfires, floods and oil spills.
